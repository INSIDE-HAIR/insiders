# Product Overview

This is a comprehensive business management platform built for InsideHair, a hair salon and beauty industry company. The platform serves salon owners, consultants, and marketing professionals with workflow automation and client management tools.

## Core Features

- **Authentication System**: Multi-provider auth (credentials, Google, GitHub) with JWT sessions
- **Drive Management**: Google Drive integration with file upload, download, and organization
- **Calendar Integration**: Google Calendar sync with event management and KPI tracking
- **Video Conferencing**: Support for Google Meet, Zoom, and Vimeo integrations
- **Marketing Salon**: Campaign management and client organization tools
- **Admin Dashboard**: User management, access control, and system administration
- **Multi-language Support**: Spanish (primary) and English localization

## Business Context

- Primary market: Hair salon and beauty industry
- Target users: Salon owners, consultants, and marketing professionals
- Focus on workflow automation and client management
- Integration with external services (Google Workspace, Zoom, etc.)

## Key Modules

- Authentication and user management
- File storage and organization (Drive)
- Calendar and appointment scheduling
- Video conferencing and training sessions
- Marketing campaign management
- Administrative tools and reporting
